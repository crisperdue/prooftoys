Prooftoys logic theorem list

This page shows the theorems of the logic as rendered by the Prooftoys/Mathtoys system. A primary value of this page is for anyone interested in seeing proofs of these theorems. To view a proof to any desired level of detail, click on one of the bits of text displayed in blue like hyperlinks.

For background, discussion, logical axioms, and description of the notation used here, please refer to the corresponding Wiki page

Note that all tautologies are also theorems of the logic.
